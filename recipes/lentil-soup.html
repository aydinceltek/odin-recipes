Lentil Soup

Home Page

Description

A hearty and nutritious soup made with tender lentils, vegetables, and warm spices. It's comforting, protein-rich, and perfect for a healthy, satisfying meal. Great served with crusty bread!

Ingredients

  • Lentils (1 cup)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Carrots (2, chopped)
  • Celery (2 stalks, chopped)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, chopped)
  • Tomato (1, chopped) or canned tomatoes (1 cup)
  • Water or vegetable broth (4 cups)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • Cumin or any spice you like (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil:

    Heat olive oil in a pot.

  2. Add vegetables:

    Add onion, carrot, celery, and garlic. Cook for 5 minutes until soft.

  3. Add lentils and broth:

    Add lentils, tomato, water or broth. Stir well.

  4. Add herbs:

    Add salt, pepper, and spices.

  5. Bring to a boil:

    Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20â€“25 minutes until lentils are soft.

  6. Taste/Adjust Seasoning:

    Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve warm.
