Cheese Pizza

Home Page

Description

A cheese pizza recipe features a classic combination of a crisp, golden crust topped with tangy tomato sauce and a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese. Simply spread sauce over rolled-out pizza dough, sprinkle with cheese, and bake until bubbly and golden. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or marinara sauce)
  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional, for brushing the crust)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning (optional)
  • All-purpose flour (for dusting the surface)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven:

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.

  2. Prepare the dough:

    Lightly flour a clean surface and roll out the pizza dough into a 10–12 inch circle (or your preferred thickness).

  3. Transfer to baking surface:

    Place the rolled dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, or a floured pizza peel if using a pizza stone.

  4. Add sauce:

    Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch around the edges for the crust.

  5. Add cheese:

    Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce. Add more cheese if you like it extra cheesy.

  6. Bake the pizza:

    Bake for 10–14 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Cool and serve:

    Let the pizza cool for a couple of minutes, then slice and enjoy!
