Description

One of the most loved foods in the world is finally here! This is a beautiful Italian Lasagna with layers of slow cooked Ragù Bolognese and Besciamella cheese sauce. Though patience is required, it is quite straight forward to make as you will see in the recipe video!

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • Olive oil (1–2 tablespoons)
  • Onion (1, finely chopped)
  • Garlic (2–3 cloves, minced)
  • Ground beef or pork (about 1 lb or 500g)
  • Crushed tomatoes (1–2 cans, about 28 oz total)
  • Tomato paste (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning (1–2 teaspoons)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

For the Cheese Mixture

  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz or 425g)
  • Egg (1, beaten)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (½ cup)
  • Salt and pepper

Other Incredients

  • Lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (2–3 cups)

Instructions

  1. Cook Meat:

    In a large skillet, heat olive oil. Brown the ground meat. Drain excess fat.

  2. Add Sauce:

    Stir in jarred pasta sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes. Break oven-ready noodles into pieces and add directly to the skillet with a splash of water (about ¼ cup). Cover and simmer for 10–12 minutes until noodles are tender.

  3. Add Cheese:

    Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, seasoning, and pepper in a bowl. Spoon dollops over the skillet, then top with mozzarella. Cover for 5 minutes, or broil briefly to melt cheese.
